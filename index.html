Life:


Leonardo da Vinci was born April 15th, 1452 in the Republic of France. He studied at the Studio of Andrea del Verrocchio. He was a well educated and well-rounded man. He had knowledge in multiple feilds of study, and use this to advance his artwork. After multiple years of producing wonderful and detailed works, he died on May 2nd, 1519 in the Kingdom of France. However, his memory will live on forever through his beautiful and inspirational work.

Leonardo da Vinci was a painter, architect, sculptor, engineer, and inventor. His works inspire millions of people o this day, and his inventions and ideas were the bluprints to many modern inventions. His contributions to the sicence community were also vast.
